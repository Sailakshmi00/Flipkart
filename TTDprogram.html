Tirumala Tirupathi A Devitional Place to Visit

                Tirupati is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.
                Its Sri Venkateswara Temple sits atop one of the the 7 peaks of Tirumala Hills,
                attracting scores of Hindu pilgrims. Sri Venkateswara National Park, home to the temple,
                also contains the Sri Venkateswara Zoological Park with lions and primates. Nearby, 
                next to a waterfall and cave believed to be sacred, 
                Sri Kapileswara Swamy Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva.
